Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines our practices for collecting, using, disclosing, and safeguarding personal information in the context of delivering cybersecurity, compliance, and risk management services.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

We collect personal information from clients and partners to provide our cybersecurity, risk assessment, and compliance services. This information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Full name and contact details
  • Organization name and designation
  • Educational and professional qualifications
  • Employment and service history
  • Business requirements and technical infrastructure details

We may use this information for the following purposes:

  • To communicate with clients about projects, services, and updates
  • To deliver, manage, and support our cybersecurity solutions
  • To ensure compliance with applicable laws, standards, and contractual obligations
  • To improve and tailor our services based on client needs and feedback

We collect and use personal information only for the purposes outlined above and obtain consent before collection or use, except where permitted or required by law.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We may disclose personal information to regulatory authorities, law enforcement agencies, or trusted partners when necessary to:

  • Fulfill our contractual commitments
